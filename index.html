About Us

The Job Placement Cell is a dedicated department or organization that focuses on assisting students or job seekers in securing employment. It provides a range of services, including career counseling, occupation guidance, interview preparation, and job search strategies.

The cell maintains partnerships with various companies and recruiters to facilitate job placements and internships. It also organizes seminars to connect candidates with potential employers. By offering tailored support and resources, the Job Placement Cell plays a crucial role in enhancing career opportunities and helping individuals achieve their professional goals.

It also offers workshops and seminars on industry trends and job market insights, equipping individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to navigate the job search rocess effectively.
